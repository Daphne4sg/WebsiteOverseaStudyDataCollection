Requirements Appendix

I. Why apply master/PhD in USA?

  1. Accessibility
  2. Cutting-edge technology
  3. Internationally society
  4. More oppurtunity
  5. Innovation and inspiration

II. Requirements

  1. GPA
  2. Letters of recommandation
  3. Resume
  4. Transcripts for your university degree or courses
  5. Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  6. GMAT or GRE scores
  7. English language test, e.g.IEITS score and TOFEL score
  8. Completed application form and application fee

III. Appendix

 i. GPA:

  GPA>3.5~4

 ii. IELTS score:

  • Standford University: Unlimited
  • Harvard University: Unlimited
  • Massachusetts Institute of Technology(MIT): IELTS: No
  • California Institute of Technology(Cal Tech): Unlimited
  • Carnegie Mellon University: 7.5

 iii. TOFEL score:

  • Standford University: 89
  • Harvard University: 80, average = 105
  • Massachusetts Institute of Technology(MIT): score > 90, average = 108
  • California Institute of Technology(Cal Tech): average = 107
  • Carnegie Mellon University: score > 102, every subject > 25, average = 105
